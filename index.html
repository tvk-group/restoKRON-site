restoKRON

A food, hospitality and merchant payment token concept for the KRON ecosystem.

restoKRON (rKRON) is designed as a digital payment and merchant infrastructure token for restaurants, cafés, hotels, delivery operators and hospitality businesses that want to explore blockchain-based payment rails, loyalty programs, settlement logic and customer rewards.

restoKRON is an early-stage token project on Base. It is not a bank, payment institution, e-money issuer, investment product or guaranteed-value asset. Any public use, merchant rollout or payment integration may require technical audits, legal review, compliance checks and regional licensing analysis.

Why restoKRON was created

restoKRON was created as a KRON ecosystem payment concept for real-world hospitality use cases: restaurants, cafés, bars, hotels, food delivery, catering, merchant settlement, customer rewards and loyalty infrastructure.

Built for merchant utility

The project is intended to support a future merchant flow where customers can pay, merchants can receive digital settlement, and the KRON ecosystem can connect tokenized rewards, QR checkout and business dashboards.

Utility Direction

Designed around hospitality, payments, rewards and merchant tools.
